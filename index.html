About Me

I’m a mechanical engineer who designs, builds, and scales real-world engineering systems—from robotics and embedded control platforms to manufacturing workflows and classroom-ready products. My work sits at the intersection of mechanical design, controls, and hands-on prototyping, with a strong focus on turning ideas into functional, testable systems.

I’ve led end-to-end projects across academic, industry, and startup environments, including robotics research systems, automated tracking platforms, and large-scale educational product deployments. I’m especially interested in systems that blend hardware, software, and human interaction, and I enjoy working in environments where rapid iteration, testing, and continuous improvement matter.
